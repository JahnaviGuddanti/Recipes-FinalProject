Egg Curry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
  • Water as needed

Instructions:

  1. Boil the Eggs: Place the eggs in a pot and cover them with water. Bring to a boil and cook for 10 minutes. Remove from heat, drain the hot water, and place the eggs in cold water. Once cooled, peel the eggs and set them aside.
  2. Prepare the Masala: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown. Add the ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the raw smell disappears. Add the pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil starts to separate from the mixture.
  3. Add Spices: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Cook for a few minutes, stirring constantly, until the spices are well incorporated.
  4. Cook the Eggs: Add the boiled eggs to the pan and gently stir to coat them with the masala. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Simmer the Curry: Add enough water to achieve your desired curry consistency. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  6. Finish the Curry: Add garam masala and salt to taste. Stir well and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with rice, naan, or roti.
