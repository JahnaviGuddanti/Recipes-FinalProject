Prawns Fry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the Marination:
  • 500 grams prawns, cleaned and deveined
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • For the Frying:
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Prawns:

    Clean and devein the prawns thoroughly. In a bowl, mix the prawns with turmeric powder, red chili powder, ginger-garlic paste, salt, and lemon juice. Let the prawns marinate for at least 30 minutes to absorb the flavors.

  2. Prepare the Frying Pan:

    Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.

  3. Sauté the Onions and Chilies:

    Add the finely chopped onions to the pan and sauté until they turn golden brown. Add the slit green chilies and curry leaves, and sauté for another minute.

  4. Cook the Prawns:

    Add the marinated prawns to the pan. Stir well to mix the prawns with the onions and spices. Cook the prawns on medium-high heat until they turn pink and are cooked through. This should take about 5-7 minutes.

  5. Add Garam Masala:

    Sprinkle the garam masala over the prawns and mix well. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to let the flavors meld together.

  6. Garnish and Serve:

    Garnish the prawns fry with freshly chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot as an appetizer or with rice and dal as a main course.
